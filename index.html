PBNJ Recipe!

Learn how to make peanut butter and jelly sandwiches!

You will need these ingredients!

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut Butter (creamy is best)
  • Jelly

Follow these steps for the best PB&J

  1. Lay out your 2 slices of bread
  2. Use a knife and spread peanut butter on one of the slices
  3. Clean knife and then use it to spread jelly on the empty slice of bread
  4. Put the bread together with the peanut butter and jelly facing eachother
